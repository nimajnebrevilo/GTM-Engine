## Outreach Strategy

### Channel Mix (Multi-touch, 3-week cadence)

**Week 1: Warm Introduction**
- Day 1: LinkedIn connection request with personalized note referencing their recent funding
- Day 3: Email #1 — Congratulations + relevant case study

**Week 2: Value Delivery**
- Day 8: Email #2 — Share Vention's State of AI 2026 report or relevant industry insight
- Day 10: LinkedIn engagement (comment on their posts, share their content)

**Week 3: Direct Ask**
- Day 15: Email #3 — Direct CTA for a 20-minute call, reference specific pain points
- Day 18: LinkedIn voice message or InMail with brief video

**Week 4: Follow-up**
- Day 22: Final email — "Did this fall off your radar?" with one-line value prop
- Day 25: Phone call (for contacts with direct dials)

### Messaging Framework by Persona

#### For CTOs (primary buyer)
**Subject lines:**
- "Congrats on the [funding round] — scaling eng next?"
- "[Company] + Vention: how [similar company] scaled from 5 to 50 engineers"
- "Quick question about your [tech stack component] roadmap"

**Opening hook:** Reference their specific funding event and connect it to the engineering scaling challenge that follows.

**Value prop:** "Vention has helped companies like ClassPass and EliseAI scale from MVP to unicorn by embedding dedicated engineering teams that work as true extensions of their in-house staff. We typically have teams operational within 2 weeks."

**Proof points:**
- ClassPass: 35+ engineers added in 2 months, MVP to unicorn
- EliseAI: 90% automation, 125% conversion lift, $75M Series D
- 500+ clients, avg relationship 36+ months

#### For VP/Head of Engineering (key influencer)
**Subject lines:**
- "How [similar company] solved their hiring bottleneck in London"
- "Dedicated teams vs. hiring in London's market — a framework"

**Opening hook:** Reference the challenge of hiring senior engineers in London's competitive market.

**Value prop:** "Rather than spending 3-6 months filling each role, Vention can provide a dedicated team of 3-10 engineers matched to your tech stack, operational within 2 weeks, at 40-60% of London hiring costs."
